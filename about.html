WE ARE ROSH MAKEOVER BEAUTY SALON AND MAKEOVER ACADEMY

We are Rosh Makeover Beauty Salon and Makeover Academy Based in Boisar Palghar, Maharashtra 401501, Since 2019.

Our academy is dedicated to providing comprehensive education and training in the field of makeovers, empowering aspiring artists to pursue their dreams and become skilled professionals in the beauty industry.
