WE ARE ROSH MAKEOVER BEAUTY SALON AND MAKEOVER ACADEMY

We are Rosh Makeover Beauty Salon and Makeover Academy Based in Boisar Palghar, Maharashtra 401501, Since 2019.

Our academy is dedicated to providing comprehensive education and training in the field of makeovers, empowering aspiring artists to pursue their dreams and become skilled professionals in the beauty industry.

Experience the ultimate in luxury at our salon, where each visit promises to make you feel truly special. Our expert stylists and estheticians are dedicated to providing you with personalized services tailored to your unique needs and preferences. Whether you're in need of a stylish haircut, a rejuvenating facial, or a relaxing massage, we offer a range of treatments using the highest-quality products and techniques. Visit us today and discover why we're the premier destination for beauty and relaxation.

  • Certified Stylists

    Our team consists of certified stylists who stay up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in hairdressing. With their expertise and creativity, you can trust them to deliver stunning results that exceed your expectations.

  • 100% Organic Cosmetics

    We believe in using only the best for our clients, which is why we exclusively use 100% organic and natural cosmetics in our treatments. These products are gentle on the skin and free from harsh chemicals, ensuring a safe and nourishing experience for all.
